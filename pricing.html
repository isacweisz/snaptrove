Questions

The things people ask before trusting us with their memories.

Do guests need an account?

Guests should be able to join a Trove quickly from an invite link or QR code, without friction.

Can guests upload for free?

Yes. Guests should upload, view, like, comment and react for free.

Who owns the photos?

You and your invited community own the memories you add. SnapTrove should never feel like it takes ownership away from you.

Can I order prints later?

Yes. A Trove should stay useful after the event, so you can come back for prints, cards, books and keepsakes.

Can I download everything?

Hosts can choose download options for their Trove, including full albums when enabled by the plan.

Is it private?

Yes. Troves are private by default and built for the people you invite.

How long are memories stored?

SnapTrove is designed as a memory vault, not a temporary upload folder. If a plan changes, memories should be archived safely rather than suddenly disappearing.

Can I create multiple Troves?

Yes. Family and future professional plans are designed for people who create memories all year round.

Can I invite unlimited guests?

Event Pass is designed for large groups where everyone should be able to join and contribute.

Can I delete my Trove?

Yes. Trove owners should control what stays, what is exported and what is deleted.
