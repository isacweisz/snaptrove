Trust & Privacy

Your memories belong to you.

Every photo you upload is private, secure and always under your control.

01

Private by default.

Every Trove starts private. Nobody can search it, stumble across it, or accidentally discover it. Only people you invite can join.

02

You own every memory.

We never own your photos, claim rights over your moments, or sell your memories. You stay in control.

03

AI that works for you.

AI helps organise, find photos, create albums and build cards. It is there to help, not to advertise.

Families first

Built with families in mind.

SnapTrove is designed for children’s birthdays, school trips, family events, weddings and private celebrations where safe sharing matters.

  • Children’s photos stay inside invited Troves.
  • Schools and families can share memories privately.
  • Guests only see the spaces they are invited to.

Control

You decide what happens inside each Trove.

Owners control who joins, who uploads, who comments, who downloads, and who leaves. Your memories never become a public feed.

Who joinsInvite by link or QR code, then manage access.
Who uploadsKeep contributions open, limited or closed after the event.
Who commentsKeep reactions and conversation inside the private Trove.
Who downloadsChoose when albums, originals and keepsakes are available.

Business model

Your data isn’t the product.

We don’t build advertising profiles. We don’t sell ads. We don’t sell your data. SnapTrove earns money from subscriptions, event passes, keepsakes and premium features, not from your personal memories.

No selling user photos No selling personal memories No public profiles No stranger algorithms

Secure storage.

Your memories are encrypted, backed up, protected and designed to stay available when you want to relive them.

Guest Access.

Guests only see the Troves they are invited to. There are no public follower counts, stranger feeds or open discovery pages.

GDPR & Privacy.

SnapTrove is being built for families, schools and businesses that need clear privacy controls and responsible handling of memories.

We’ll keep the language simple, the controls clear, and the support human.
