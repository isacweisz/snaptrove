About SnapTrove

We believe memories deserve better.

The best moments in life are captured by everyone, then scattered across phones, chats and forgotten camera rolls. SnapTrove exists to bring those shared memories home.

Create Your First TroveTalk to us

Why we exist

Every event creates a story. Too often, that story gets lost.

Weddings, birthdays, holidays, school trips, camps and family gatherings are full of moments that matter. But the photos live on different phones. The best clips stay in group chats. Grandparents, parents, friends and guests all hold part of the story, but rarely the whole thing.

SnapTrove is built around a simple belief: private memories should be easy to collect, beautiful to relive and meaningful to keep.

01

For the people who were there

SnapTrove is not another public feed. Each Trove is a private space for the guests, families, teams or communities who shared the moment.

02

For family history

Today’s birthday photos become tomorrow’s family stories. SnapTrove helps preserve the little moments that become part of who we are.

03

For memories you can hold

Photos should become more than pixels. They should become albums, cards, books, prints and keepsakes that live beyond the screen.

Our vision

The private social platform for life’s memories.

Instagram is for public moments. SnapTrove is for the memories shared with the people who were actually there. Our long-term vision is a beautiful home for every celebration, milestone, trip, school year, season and family story.
