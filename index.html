Talk to Us

FAQ

Everything people ask before trusting an app with their photos.

Is SnapTrove private?

Yes. Troves are invite-only by default, with sharing controls for every event and no public algorithmic feed.

Is SnapTrove trying to replace Instagram?

No. SnapTrove adds Instagram-style social features inside private Troves. It follows memories, not strangers.

Do guests need an account?

The product goal is simple guest upload links, with accounts only when needed.

Do guests pay to join?

No. Guests should be able to join by QR or invite link, upload, view, like, comment, react and save favourites for free.

What requires payment?

Creating Troves, full-resolution downloads, whole-album downloads, AI cards, AI reels, AI books, face search, print orders and business features.

What makes SnapTrove grow?

Guests join free, AI finds photos of them, they share branded reels/cards, then SnapTrove prompts them to create their own Trove.

Can guests like and comment?

Yes. Each Trove is designed as a private social feed with likes, comments, replies, mentions, emoji reactions, Stories, favourites and pinned photos.

Can AI recognise faces?

Yes. SnapTrove is designed to group people, moments, duplicates, best photos and photo quality.

Can I make cards?

Yes. Create birthday, thank-you, wedding, Bar/Bat Mitzvah, anniversary, congratulations and holiday cards to download, share or print by post.

Can I order books and prints?

Yes. Books, cards, posters, calendars, memory boxes, wall art, event newspapers and prints are core to the product plan.

Can I delete photos?

Yes. Owners should be able to remove photos and control what gets shared or printed.

How many guests can join?

The Event plan is built for unlimited guests at one event.
