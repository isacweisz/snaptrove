FAQ

The questions every guest, parent, organiser, and client will ask.

Is it private?

Yes. Troves are invite-only by default, with sharing controls for each event.

Do guests need an account?

The goal is simple guest upload links, with accounts only when needed.

Can AI recognise faces?

Yes. SnapTrove is designed to group people, moments, duplicates, and quality.

Can I order books?

Yes. Books, cards, posters, booklets, and prints are part of the product plan.

Can I delete photos?

Yes. Owners should be able to remove photos and control what gets shared.

How many guests?

The Event plan is built for unlimited guests at one event.
